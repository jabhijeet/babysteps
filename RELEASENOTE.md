## Version 1.0.0 (Initial Release)
**Date: May 13, 2026**

We are excited to announce the initial release of BabySteps! Our goal is to provide parents with a secure, private, and intelligent way to track their newborn's health and routine.

### 🌟 Highlights

#### 👶 Comprehensive Baby Management
*   Create multiple baby profiles with personalized details.
*   Securely store names, birth dates, and gender with AES-256 encryption.

#### 🍼 Advanced Feeding Logs
*   **Dual-Timer Breastfeeding**: Track nursing duration for both sides independently.
*   **Volume Tracking**: Precise logging for bottle feeds (Formula/Expressed Milk) in ml or oz.
*   **Insights**: Real-time feeding trends and daily volume summaries.

#### 💤 Sleep & Wake Windows
*   Automatic calculation of wake windows to help predict the next nap.
*   Visual sleep patterns to understand your baby's circadian rhythm.

#### 📉 WHO Growth Integration
*   Plot Weight, Height, and Head Circumference.
*   Compare data against official WHO Growth Charts (Percentiles) with continuous curve rendering.

#### 🧷 Hygiene & Health
*   Quick-toggle diaper logging (Wet/Dirty/Both).
*   Detailed stool analysis for color and consistency monitoring.
*   Medical log for vaccinations and medication reminders.

#### 🎙️ AI Voice Interface (Beta)
*   "Hands-free" logging via natural language voice commands.
*   Secure, on-device transcription to maintain privacy.

#### 🔒 Security & Sync
*   **Privacy-by-Design**: All data stays on your device by default.
*   **Google Drive Sync**: Seamless backup to your own private Google Drive account.
*   **Secure Storage**: Encryption keys are protected by hardware-backed keystores.

*   Targeting Android SDK 35 for latest security and performance standards.
